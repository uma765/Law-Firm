About Our Firm

The Ledger Law Firm is a nationally recognized personal injury law firm with offices in California, Texas, and Washington. Our attorneys are licensed in multiple states and handle cases nationwide.

We specialize in helping injury victims recover maximum compensation for their losses, including medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering. Our team has decades of combined experience fighting insurance companies and corporations.

We operate on a contingency fee basis, meaning you pay nothing unless we win your case. Your initial consultation is always free.
